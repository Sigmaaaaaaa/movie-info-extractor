Avatar is a 2009 science fiction film directed by James Cameron. Set in the mid-22nd century, it follows humanity’s colonization of the lush alien moon Pandora, where conflicts arise between corporate interests and the indigenous Na’vi people. The film is renowned for its groundbreaking visual effects and pioneering use of 3D technology.

The Dark Knight is a 2008 superhero film directed by Christopher Nolan. It is widely regarded as one of the most influential modern films in the superhero genre, noted for its grounded realism, moral complexity, and the iconic performance of Heath Ledger as the Joker. The film reshaped expectations for comic-book adaptations and became a landmark in blockbuster filmmaking.

Inception is a 2010 science-fiction thriller film written and directed by Christopher Nolan. It explores the concept of shared dreaming and subconscious manipulation through high-concept visual storytelling and complex narrative layers. The film is widely regarded for its originality, technical mastery, and philosophical depth.

Interstellar is a 2014 science-fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. Set in a near-future Earth ravaged by ecological collapse, it follows astronauts who travel through a wormhole in search of a new habitable planet. The film is noted for its combination of emotional storytelling and scientifically grounded depictions of space travel.
